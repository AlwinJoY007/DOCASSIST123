How It Works

From Documents to Answers in 4 Steps

ClinRAG transforms raw medical literature into structured AI knowledge, ready for clinical queries.

01

Upload Medical Papers

Upload PDFs of research papers, clinical guidelines, or import directly from PubMed.

02

Gemini AI Embeddings

Documents are converted into multi-modal embeddings using Google Gemini AI, capturing deep clinical context.

03

Pinecone Vector Storage

Embeddings are stored in Pinecone's serverless vector database for ultra-low latency RAG search.

04

Ask & Get Answers

Doctors ask clinical questions and receive evidence-based answers with cited sources in seconds.
